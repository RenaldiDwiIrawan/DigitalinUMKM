Create a warm and inviting landing page for a cozy cafe called 'Aroma Senja Cafe' using Next.js and Tailwind CSS.

Design requirements:
- Color Palette: Earthy tones (Brown #6F4E37, Cream #EADCC3, Off-white #FDFBF7, Dark Wood #4A3728).
- Typography: Elegant Serif for headings (e.g., Playfair Display style) and clean Sans-serif for body text.
- Navigation: Minimalist sticky navbar with 'Menu', 'About', and 'Location' links.

Sections:
- Hero: Large evocative headline 'Seduhan Hangat di Ujung Senja' with a prominent 'Order Now' button.
- Signature Menu: 3 cards featuring top items (Kopi Senja, V60, Croissant) with prices and descriptions.
- About: Brief story about roasted coffee and heritage.
- Visit Us: Opening hours and Bekasi location info.
- Footer: Warm, dark theme with copyright and location.

Aesthetic: Rustic, minimalist, high-end coffee shop feel.
